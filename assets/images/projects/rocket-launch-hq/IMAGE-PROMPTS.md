## Export notes

1. Generate images with Cursor (or your preferred tool) using the prompts above.
2. Save into `assets/images/projects/rocket-launch-hq/` with the **exact filenames** listed.
3. Recommended export sizes (adjust if your tool prefers other dimensions):
   - Gallery 16:9: ~1920×1080
   - Gallery 4:3: ~1600×1200
   - Gallery 1:1: ~1200×1200
   - Gallery portrait 3:4: ~1200×1600
4. Convert to WebP if needed (ImageMagick or cwebp).
5. Until backgrounds are wired (Loop 3+), gallery surfaces may appear empty (expected).
